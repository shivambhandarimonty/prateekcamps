The Ancient Legacy

Kedarnath is more than just a pilgrimage; it is a timeless story of the Pandavas seeking redemption. Legend says Shiva took the form of a bull to hide, but when Bheema recognized him, he dived into the earth, leaving his hump as the holy Jyotirlinga.

The temple has stood for over a thousand years, built with colossal stone slabs. Even in the 2013 floods, it remained untouched, protected by the divine **Bheem Shila** rock.
